Hermit Hero

Turn Based, RPG, Adventure

Platform :

30% Progress

You are a legendary knight, even though you have decided to retire and want to live a normal life like ordinary people. Get married, have children and live peacefully in the depths of the forest alone with your lover. However, the title he once held turned into a threat, while you were hunting in the forest, without you realizing it, your wife had been kidnapped by someone unknown and took her away. The kidnapper left a letter containing the words "Find me, and fight me. So I will return your wife."
